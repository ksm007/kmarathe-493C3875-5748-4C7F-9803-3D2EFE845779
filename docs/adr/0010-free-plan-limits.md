# 0010: Free plan limits

New organizations start on a free plan with hard product limits, keeping adoption friction low while bounding operating cost before billing is added.

Free plan limits:
- 20 seats per organization
- 3 AI calls per user per day
- 1 active sprint
- 5 planned sprints
- 500 open tasks
- 100 MB image attachment storage
- unlimited completed sprints

Accepted memberships and pending invitations both consume seats. Completed tasks remain as history, but open tasks are limited because they drive the active board, search, AI retrieval, and drag-and-drop load.
